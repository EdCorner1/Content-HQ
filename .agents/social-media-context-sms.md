## Tone & Voice
- Casual but good energy
- Conversational, like talking to a mate
- Self-deprecating humour works well
- Honest and specific — real moments over polished scripts
- Never sounds like an ad — brand comes in late, naturally
- "Human in the loop" — values authenticity even when using AI

## Content Style
- Hook on the problem or result, NOT the brand
- Real story first, product appears naturally
- Messier sentences, real thoughts, stuff you'd actually say
- Short CTAs — no fluff
- 40–75 seconds ideal for short-form

## What Works
- Specific moments (checking phone at 6pm, waking up to leads)
- Admitting weakness before showing the solution
- "I didn't ask it to do this — it just did"
- Solo founder pain: too busy building to do outreach
- Clawbite: daily agent updates, autopilot features, site audits
- Detris: leads while sleeping/on beach, 300 messages, imposter syndrome

## What Doesn't Work
- Mentioning the brand too early (within first 3 seconds)
- Sounding like a feature list
- Over-polished, structured scripts
- Generic hooks ("here's what I learned about...")
